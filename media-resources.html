Articles & Research

Dr. Lombard's research spans pediatric hypnosis, atopic conditions, food allergy, and children's mental health.

Book Chapter

Medical Trauma

Lombard, L., Polloni, L. · Encyclopedia of Food Allergy, vol. 1, pp. 655–665. Elsevier, 2024

An overview of medical trauma in the context of food allergy — its presentation, psychological impact, and clinical implications.

 Book Chapter

Hypnosis with Children and Adolescents

Lombard, L., & Milling, L.S. · Evidence-Based Practice in Clinical Hypnosis. American Psychological Association, 2023

A chapter in the APA's landmark volume on evidence-based practice in clinical hypnosis — covering how to adapt hypnotherapeutic approaches for younger populations.

Knibb R.C., Herbert L.J., Jones C.J., … Lombard L., et al. · Allergy, 2024

Kan K., Enaholo O., Kanaley M., … Lombard L., Gupta R. · 2023

A multi-site study examining the mental health, social, and academic impacts of the pandemic on Chicago families in disproportionately affected neighborhoods.

Ezeoke O., Kanaley M., Brown D., … Lombard L., Gupta R. · 2022

Examines how the pandemic disrupted adolescent development, peer relationships, and access to care in the Chicago metropolitan area.

 J Allergy Clin Immunol Pract

The Psychosocial Burden of Food Allergy Among Adults: A US Population-Based Study

Warren C., Dyer A., Lombard L., Dunn-Galvin A., Gupta R. · 2021

A national study documenting the anxiety, quality-of-life impact, and psychosocial challenges faced by adults living with food allergy in the United States.

 JAMA Network Open

Raviv T., Warren C., Washburn J., … Lombard L., Gupta R. · 2021

A large-scale study on gaps in children's access to mental health services during the pandemic, and which populations faced the greatest disparities.

 Annals of Allergy

Brown D., Negris O., Gupta R., … Lombard L. · 2021

Investigates the prevalence and psychosocial consequences of food allergy bullying, with implications for school-based intervention.

Bozen A., Zaslavsky J., … Lombard L., Gupta R. · 2020

Identifies economic and structural barriers that prevent low-income families from effectively managing food allergy, with policy implications.

 Annals of Allergy

Pediatric Allergists' Perspectives on the Psychosocial Challenges of Food Allergy and Factors that Support Coping

Bozen A., Kanaley M., Negris O., Gupta R., Lombard L. · Ann Allergy Asthma Immunol, 2020

Explores how pediatric allergists perceive and address the psychosocial burden of food allergy, and what factors they identify as supporting patient coping.
